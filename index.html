Dr. Fang-Chung Chen is a professor in Department of Photonics, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (National Chiao Tung University).

He is currently the Chairman of Department of Photonics.

He has published more than 130 SCI papers. His H-index is 46 (Google Scholar); 40 (Web of Science).
